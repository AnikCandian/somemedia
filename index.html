A Short Memorial of My Parents

On this very day a few years ago, my mom and dad passed away from an auto wreck. It just happened abruptly. I can remember the moments where I shot ducks with my old man and conversed with my mother. And so much more. Yet, they're gone, never to be seen again. I hope they're doing well wherever they are now.
